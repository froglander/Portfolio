About Me

I have long been fascinated by computers. I remember in middle school or high school, while staring at a DOS prompt, wanting to learn how to tell computers what to do. It has been a long and winding road but web development is where I would like to focus. I am passionate about usability and making web sites and apps intuitive and easy to use. Originally from Washington state, I have a degree in Management Information Systems from Western Washington University.

I moved to Florida in September of 2009 after meeting my now husband in World of Warcraft. We started off in south Florida and moved to the Orlando area last year. We have settled on 5 acres out west in Clermont where we live with our two dogs, two cats and a horse.

I love to read. Currently I am rereading The Dresden Files books by Jim Butcher. Another favorite that I have read many times (and can't wait for Book 3 to be out) are The Kingkiller Chronicles by Patrick Rothfuss.
